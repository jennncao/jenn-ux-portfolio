HEY THERE!

I'm Jennifer Cao - Jenn for short!

I'm a recent interaction design graduate from the University of Pittsburgh based in the New York Metropolitan Area. I studied human interaction in multiple areas of technology including websites, podcasts, video games, and more. During my time at school, I learned of concepts and theories of the UX design process as well as the software engineering process.

I've always loved creating - from traditional art to crafts to interactive technology, which bleeds into my desire to create usable interfaces. Interests in art and game design (among others) influence my personal and design identity.

In my free time, I like to catch up on my video game / reading / to-watch list, visit my favorite bagel place, and make failed bread attempts.
